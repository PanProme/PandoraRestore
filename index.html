Pillar 3: Energy Metabolism

Pillar 4: Perfusion & Angiogenesis

Actives: L-Citrulline (0.75%), Adenosine (dual role)

Outcome: 20-40% improvement in dermal blood flow, new capillary formation in ischemic areas, prevents hypoxia-triggered catagen

Technology Stack

Pandora's competitive moat is built on two validated platforms: VF-CIJ manufacturing and PrometheusAgentic GTM intelligence.

1. VF-CIJ Manufacturing Platform

Pandora VF-CIJ is a continuous-flow manufacturing system for lipid nanoparticles. It rapidly mixes lipids dissolved in ethanol with aqueous buffer in milliseconds using high-velocity jets, causing self-assembly into nano-sized particles. Immediately after mixing, inline vacuum flash evaporation removes 70-90% of ethanol at low pressure. Evaporated ethanol is condensed, purified through molecular sieves, and recycled—cutting solvent waste by 80% compared to traditional methods.

How It Works

Step 1: Rapid Mixing – High-velocity jets create millisecond contact between lipid and aqueous streams, triggering nano-assembly
Step 2: Inline Solvent Removal – Vacuum flash evaporation (100-200 mbar, 30-42°C) removes most ethanol in seconds, not hours
Step 3: Ethanol Recovery – Two-stage condensation captures ≥99.5% vapor; molecular sieve purifies for reuse
Result: Drastically reduced downstream processing time and costs. 4.8M liters/year capacity at 50 L/min nameplate.

MLOps: Smart Autopilot for Manufacturing

Real-Time Monitoring: Sensors track Raman spectroscopy, refractive index, conductivity, temperature, flow rates, vacuum pressure
Quality Prediction: Predicts particle size, uniformity, encapsulation efficiency before lab tests confirm them
Adaptive Adjustments: When confident, makes small, safe changes to inlet temps or vacuum pressure to keep product centered in quality target
Learning: After each batch, updates understanding of how lipid formulations behave and suggests improved starting conditions
Result: Faster startup (2+ min saved/batch), fewer rejections (diversions cut 50%), tighter consistency, lower costs. FDA-compliant logging (21 CFR Part 11).

2. PrometheusAgentic GTM Platform

PrometheusAgentic is a go-to-market and market intelligence platform answering three questions: who to focus on, why now, and what to say. Instead of relying on expensive data feeds, it builds a "mosaic" of independent signals—search trends, reviews, press, sales data, influencer activity—each tagged with source, confidence, and recency. When signals agree, confidence rises; when they conflict, the system surfaces disagreement rather than hiding it.
